# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
People, creators, teams, and small businesses who want a quick physical way to share a live digital identity through NFC cards, medals, and key tags.

## Product Purpose
LinkUp connects physical NFC products to public profile pages so someone can tap or scan a product and instantly open the owner's live identity, links, and contact flow.

## Brand Personality
Neon, direct, kinetic. The experience should feel fast, confident, and tech-forward without becoming noisy or hard to read.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic SaaS landing sections, pale card grids, decorative-only tech patterns, and shop blocks that feel disconnected from the dark LinkUp visual system.

## Design Principles
Show the physical product clearly.
Keep actions direct and visible.
Use motion to imply tap-and-scan speed, while respecting reduced-motion settings.
Protect the black-and-cyan identity already established in the codebase.
Make the shop feel like part of the brand story, not a separate ecommerce template.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target readable contrast on dark backgrounds, keyboard-visible focus states, descriptive image alt text, and reduced-motion fallbacks for autoplay and animated effects.
